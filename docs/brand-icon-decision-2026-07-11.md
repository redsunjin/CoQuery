# CoQuery Brand Icon Decision

Date: 2026-07-11
Status: Approved

## Selected Direction

CoQuery uses the v4 Liquid Glass SQL-token icon as its primary brand icon.

- Master artwork: `app_store_assets/brand/coquery-liquid-glass-sql-tokens-master-v4.png`
- 1024px approved icon: `app_store_assets/brand/coquery-liquid-glass-sql-tokens-app-icon-1024-v4.png`
- Xcode app icon: `ios/App/App/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/AppIcon-512@2x.png`
- App Store registration icon: `app_store_assets/ios-registration/icons/app-icon-1024.png`

## Brand Metaphor

- The large `Q` represents Query.
- The three internal token lines suggest `SELECT`, `FROM`, and `WHERE` without relying on literal text.
- The blue token represents a selected table, field, or matched result.
- The straight terminal tail communicates execution while preserving a modern geometric silhouette.
- Liquid Glass is limited to the material treatment; the underlying geometry remains direct and non-organic.

## iOS Asset Boundary

The approved 1024px PNG is square, opaque, and fills the canvas. It does not bake a rounded app-icon mask into the source artwork. iOS applies the platform mask at display time.

The existing splash assets are not part of this decision and remain unchanged until a separate launch-screen composition is approved.
